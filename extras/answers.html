SRAM Quiz Answer Key

  1. What is a primary characteristic of SRAM concerning power removal?
    Correct Answer: B) Volatile; memory is lost when power is removed.

  2. How does SRAM compare to Flash memory in terms of speed and cost?
    Correct Answer: C) SRAM is faster and more expensive than Flash memory.

  3. What is the primary use of SRAM in the ATtiny1626 microcontroller, as stated in the text?
    Correct Answer: B) To store variables and temporary data.

  4. What is the typical access granularity for SRAM, meaning how it can be written to or read from?
    Correct Answer: C) It allows access to individual bytes.

  5. According to the text, what is the specified size of SRAM in the AVR ATtiny1626 microcontroller?
    Correct Answer: B) 2 KB.

  6. How are SRAM and I/O generally accessed on the AVR Core?
    Correct Answer: B) Via memory-mapped I/O (MMIO), accessing them through the data space.

  7. In the AVR Core, where do stack operations like push and pop primarily occur, and at what starting address?
    Correct Answer: C) In SRAM, starting at 0x3800.
